Quick Preparation Tips

🔬

Research the Company

Spend 30 minutes studying the company's recent work, values, and the role's requirements before every interview.

🪞

Practice Out Loud

Record yourself answering common questions. Reviewing playback dramatically improves delivery and confidence.

❓

Prepare Your Questions

Always have 2–3 thoughtful questions ready for the interviewer. It shows genuine interest and preparation.
